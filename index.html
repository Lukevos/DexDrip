DexDrip

This is my DexDrip Project page! Its used to catch raw transmissions from your Dexcom transmitter and then, using a calibration algorithm written by me (as in, you shouldn't trust it) give you a nice realtime graph of your BG levels.

ITS A WORK IN PROGGRESS

  • There are no notifications
  • There is no uploading
  • There is very little error handling
  • There is bad behavior around slope generation after missed readings
  • Im not even beginning to use the filtered data for error handling yet
  • It has NOT been tested on the first few days of a sensor so it will likely be WAY OFF
